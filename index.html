    Highlights / Events

    The Indian Cyber Crime Coordination Centre (I4C) is an initiative by the Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India, to tackle cybercrime comprehensively. Launched in 2018, I4C coordinates law enforcement efforts across the country and improves capabilities to handle cybercrime through modern technology and data analysis.

    The Union Home Secretary unveiled the first edition of I4C newsletter 'Cyber Pravaha'. It's an initiative to share precise information related to Cybercrime with Law Enforcement Agencies and other stakeholders.
